What is Windstream Kinetic?

Windstream Kinetic is the brand name of Windstream’s internet and TV services, similar to Comcast’s Xfinity or Charter’s Spectrum services. Windstream Kinetic is available in select areas of 18 states. Availability, pricing and speeds for Windstream Kinetic vary by location.
